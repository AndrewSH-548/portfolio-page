Retrospective

This project was tougher than I anticipated. Due to the necessary information all being in different JSONs, multiple data requests end up lagging the site a fair bit. Additionally, with how I configured alternate forms, I had to hardcode a ton of workarounds for cases like special characters in pokémon names, hyphens applying to alternate forms but not to paradox forms, and so on.
